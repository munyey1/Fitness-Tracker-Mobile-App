FitLog App Guide

The FitLog app is a logging app that helps track your gym workouts. Choose from a plethora of exercises to vary your workouts.


Home Screen

This home screen is where you can view your workouts that you have added for the day. From this page, you can also add some new workouts by clicking on the blue circle. Furthermore, you can enter the settings and the guide.

Click on these buttons to get more information.


Adding a Workout

Muscle Group Page

When you click on the blue circle, you will be taken to the muscle groups page. This is where you can view all of the possible muscle groups that you can add exercises for. When you click on one of these muscle groups, you will be taken to the exercises page.


Exercises Page

On the exercises page, you can view all of the possible exercises that you can add to your workout. When you click on one of these exercises, you will be taken to a page where you can add such exercise.


Adding an Exercise Page

On this page, you can add the exercise to your workout. You can alter the weight and reps for the exercise, whilst also adding the number of sets that you want to do. When you enter the weight and reps, clicking the "Save" button will add a set to the exercise.

When you are finished, you can go back to the home page by going back.


Settings

On the settings page, you can change the notifications settings. Either they are on or off. When they are on, you will be notified at 12:00pm every day.


Share

When you click the share button, the share options will appear, where you can share with other users that you have worked out today.


Guide

Clicking the guide button will show this page!
